Privacy Policy

How ATSPro handles your data — and why we believe privacy is a right, not a feature

Last Updated: April 4, 2026  |  Effective Date: April 4, 2026  |  Contact: support@atspro.in

🔒 Privacy Summary: ATSPro processes your resume entirely in your browser. Your resume file and its contents are NEVER sent to any server. We do not store, share, or sell any personal data from your resume. The only data we may collect is standard website analytics (page views, country, device type) with no personally identifiable information.

1. Who We Are

ATSPro ("we," "us," "our") is a free online tool that helps job seekers analyze their resumes against ATS (Applicant Tracking System) criteria. The service is accessible at the domain where you found this privacy policy. Our contact email is support@atspro.in.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect (very little), how we use it (minimally), and your rights with respect to that data.

2. Personal Data — What We Collect

We collect the absolute minimum necessary to operate the website.

2.1 Resume Content — NOT collected

When you upload a resume to ATSPro, the file is processed using client-side JavaScript (PDF.js and Mammoth.js libraries) running directly in your browser. The resume content is analyzed locally and the results are displayed to you. No part of your resume — not the file itself, not the extracted text, not the analysis results — is ever transmitted to our servers or stored anywhere by ATSPro.

2.2 Job Description Text — NOT collected

When you paste a job description into the Job Description Matcher, that text is also processed entirely in your browser. It is not sent to any server.

2.3 Contact Form Data

If you use our contact form to reach out to us, we collect your name, email address, and message content. This information is used only to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties or used for marketing without your explicit consent.

2.4 Analytics Data

We may use privacy-respecting analytics (such as aggregated page view counts) to understand how the website is used. This data is anonymized and cannot be linked to any individual. We do not use Google Analytics or other invasive tracking tools on our tool pages.

3. Cookies

ATSPro uses minimal cookies. Specifically:

  • No tracking cookies are placed on visitors to our main resume analysis tool.
  • Blog pages may load content from Google Fonts or use basic session cookies for functionality. These do not track personal behavior.
  • We do not use cookies for advertising targeting, behavioral profiling, or cross-site tracking.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting the core resume analysis functionality of ATSPro.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website loads resources from the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) — serves web fonts. Google may log the request (IP, browser type). No personal data from your resume is involved.
  • cdnjs.cloudflare.com — serves the PDF.js and Mammoth.js JavaScript libraries. Cloudflare may log standard request data.
  • GitHub Pages / Hosting Provider — hosts the static website files. Standard server logs may include your IP address.

We do not use third-party advertising networks, social media tracking pixels, or user profiling services on our core tooling pages.

5. How We Use Data

The very limited data we do collect is used only to:

  • Respond to contact form inquiries
  • Understand aggregate website traffic patterns (how many people visit, from which countries, on which pages)
  • Improve the functionality and user experience of the website
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share it with third parties except as required by law or as described in this policy.

6. Data Retention

Since we do not collect resume or personal analysis data, there is nothing to retain from your tool usage. Contact form messages are retained for a maximum of 12 months, after which they are permanently deleted. Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely as it contains no personal information.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have various rights with respect to your personal data, including:

  • Right of Access — You can request what personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification — You can request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure — You can request deletion of personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Object — You can object to processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability — You can request your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@atspro.in. We will respond within 30 days.

Note: Since we do not collect or store resume data, most data rights requests will have nothing to act upon for the core tool usage. For contact form queries, we can provide, correct, or delete any message you've sent us.

8. Children's Privacy

ATSPro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information to us, please contact us at support@atspro.in and we will delete it immediately.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect the limited data we collect. Since resume analysis happens in your browser, the most sensitive data — your resume content — never travels over the internet to us, which is the highest possible level of security we could provide for that data.

Contact form submissions are transmitted over HTTPS encryption. We regularly review our security practices to ensure they remain appropriate.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will add a notice on the homepage. Your continued use of ATSPro after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
