Frequently Asked Questions

Is ATSPro really free to use? +

Yes, 100% free. We don't have any paid plans, hidden charges, or premium tiers. The ATS score checker, Job Description Matcher, and all 10 analysis categories are completely free. We fund ATSPro through non-intrusive advertising on the blog.

Is my resume really private? Does ATSPro store my data? +

Absolutely. When you upload your resume, it is processed entirely within your browser using JavaScript. The file and its contents are never sent to any server. We don't have a backend database storing resume content. Zero bytes of your resume are stored anywhere by us. You can verify this by watching your browser's Network tab — no upload requests are made.

What file formats does ATSPro support? +

ATSPro supports PDF (text-based, not scanned), DOCX, DOC, and TXT files up to 10MB. If your PDF was created by scanning a physical document (image PDF), our parser cannot read it — please export your resume from Word or Google Docs as a text-based PDF instead.

How accurate is the ATS score? +

Our scoring engine is built from research into real ATS platforms including Workday, Taleo, Greenhouse, iCIMS, and Lever. While no tool can replicate every proprietary ATS algorithm exactly, our 9-category scoring framework covers the most important factors that all major ATS systems evaluate. The score gives you a reliable directional guide — a higher score consistently correlates with better ATS pass-through rates.

Can I use ATSPro for multiple resumes or job applications? +

Yes! There's no limit. You can analyze as many resumes as you want, as many times as you want. Many users keep multiple versions of their resume tailored to different roles and use ATSPro's Job Description Matcher to check each version against the specific job posting before applying.
