Expert Advice

ATS Best Practices

Proven tips to maximize your ATS score

📝

Use Standard Section Headers

ATS systems look for exact keywords like "Work Experience", "Education", "Skills". Avoid creative names like "My Journey".

High Impact
🎯

Mirror Job Description Keywords

Copy exact phrases from the job posting into your resume. ATS systems match verbatim keywords, not synonyms.

High Impact
📊

Quantify Achievements

Use numbers everywhere: "Increased sales by 40%", "Managed team of 12 engineers", "Reduced costs by $50K".

High Impact
🔤

Use Action Verbs

Start bullet points with strong verbs: Led, Developed, Implemented, Achieved, Optimized, Designed, Delivered.

Medium Impact
📋

Choose Simple Formatting

Avoid tables, text boxes, headers/footers, images, and multi-column layouts. ATS can't read complex formatting.

High Impact
📧

Complete Contact Information

Always include: name, phone, professional email, LinkedIn URL, and city/state (full address not required).

Medium Impact
